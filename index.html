iPhone app

Save the tickets worth remembering.

recu helps you capture, crop, enhance, organize, and revisit tickets from movies, concerts, trips, games, and ordinary days that quietly matter.

About recu

recu is designed for personal ticket archiving. It keeps ticket images and notes on your device by default, supports optional saving to Photos, and offers a Pro unlock for unlimited saved tickets.

Scan and crop

Use the camera or photo library, then adjust the ticket edges before saving.

Keep it local

Your ticket images, notes, folders, and preferences stay on your device by default.

Share memories

Create simple ticket cards and share only what you choose through iOS sharing.
