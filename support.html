Frequently asked questions

Where are my tickets saved?

By default, ticket images and metadata are stored locally inside the app on your iPhone.

Will deleting the app remove my tickets?

Deleting the app removes local app data. Images that you separately saved to the system Photos library remain in Photos.

How do I restore Pro?

Open recu, go to Settings, then choose Restore Purchases in the payment section.

Does recu upload my ticket images?

No. recu does not upload your ticket images, notes, folders, or preferences.
