Privacy Policy for recu

recu is built as a private, personal ticket archive. The app does not require an account, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Information we do not collect

recu does not collect, upload, sell, or share your ticket images, notes, folders, app preferences, or other personal content.

Local storage

Ticket images, thumbnails, notes, favorites, folders, and preferences are stored on your device by default. If you delete the app, locally stored app data is deleted as part of iOS app removal.

Photos and camera access

Camera access is used only to capture ticket images. Photo library access is used only to import images you choose and read image dates when available. If you enable "Save to Photos", recu uses add-only photo permission to save the final ticket image to your system photo library.

Motion access

Motion sensor access is used only for the subtle tilt effect on ticket cards inside the app.

In-App Purchases

recu offers a Pro unlock through Apple's App Store In-App Purchase system. Payment, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple. recu reads purchase entitlement status only to determine whether unlimited saving is unlocked.

Sharing

When you use iOS sharing features, you decide what to share and with whom. recu does not automatically share your content with third parties.

Contact
